Welcome to SwiftPreview 2.0!


Control key pins previews. Learn more

We're delighted to announce that SwiftPreview has successfully upgraded to version 2.0! This is a huge release and implements the largest feature requests from our users. You can now close this window, or look below to review the new features, and customize when and how previews show up. By default, we'll always show a preview after hovering on a link for a short amount of time. You can pin the preview to the screen for easy reading by tapping the control key. Also, if you'd prefer not to see so many previews, you can set previews to appear only while pressing the shift key.

Faster, more beautiful, dynamic previews

Previews now dynamically resize to give you the best viewing experience. The preview moves around your mouse cursor to intelligently move out of the way when you're ready to move on to knew content.

Pin the Preview

Now you can freeze the preview in place by pressing the Control key. Press the control key when you want to keep the preview up on the screen, even while moving the mouse off of the link.

Scroll preview contents

When the preview is pinned, you can scroll through the content just like on a normal window. This allows you to efficiently manage consumption of high-levels of content.

Facebook Photo Zoom

Hover over photos on Facebook to show a beautiful full-sized, dynamically scaled preview!

Internet-wide Photo Zoom

Photo zooming works across all sites -- not just Facebook!

Watch YouTube videos in previews

Hover over a youtube video URL to launch a preview, which you can pin and watch.

Instant previews

Previews now render instantly using Chrome prerendering. Option to disable to save bandwidth is in the settings.

Prevent common file types from downloading on hover

Now we won't download common files types when you hover over them.
